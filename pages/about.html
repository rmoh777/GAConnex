About GA Connects

Working to bridge the digital divide in Georgia

Our Mission

GA Connects is dedicated to ensuring that every Georgian has access to affordable, reliable internet service. We believe that internet access is not a luxury, but a necessity for education, healthcare, employment, and civic participation.

What We Do

  • Connect eligible households with affordable internet service
  • Provide digital literacy training and resources
  • Advocate for policies that expand broadband access
  • Partner with communities to identify and address connectivity needs
