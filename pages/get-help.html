Get Help with Internet Access

We're here to help you get connected

Available Programs

We offer several programs to help you get affordable internet access:

Affordable Connectivity Program (ACP)

The ACP provides a discount of up to $30 per month toward internet service for eligible households. Some households may qualify for up to $75 per month if they are on qualifying Tribal lands.

Lifeline Program

Lifeline provides a monthly discount on phone or internet service for eligible low-income consumers.

How to Apply

  1. Check your eligibility using our online tool
  2. Gather required documentation (ID, proof of income, etc.)
  3. Complete the application form
  4. We'll help you choose the best plan for your needs
